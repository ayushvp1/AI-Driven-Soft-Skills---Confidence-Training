🪞 Mirror Talk Exercise

Practice speaking to yourself with confidence. Use the camera as your mirror.

📷 Click "Start Camera" to begin

How to Practice

  1. Start the camera and position yourself in frame
  2. Make eye contact with yourself (look at the camera)
  3. Speak out loud about your day, goals, or any topic
  4. Notice any discomfort and breathe through it
  5. End by saying one genuine compliment to yourself

💡 Speaking Prompts

Tell yourself about your day
Describe 3 things you're grateful for
Practice your self-introduction
Say 3 positive affirmations
Explain something you learned recently
